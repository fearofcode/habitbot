Change your life, one day at a time

Habitbot is a simple, free application that lets you track goals you want to do on a regular, recurring basis, like exercising or working on a side project. Habitbot will track streaks of keeping up the goals you set out on so that you can look back and be proud of what you've accomplished so far in the hopes that you'll continue the progress you've already made.

Don’t break the chain

Habitbot motivates you to keep good habits by tracking streaks of positive progress. This is inspired by a motivational technique Jerry Seinfeld allegedly uses to keep producing good work.

Take little steps

You can set goals you want to accomplish every day, or on specific days of the week, so that getting started is easy and so is every additional step. Before you know it, you'll have a better life.

Natural language input

Habitbot lets you type in goals in plain English, like:

  • go for a walk every day
  • go to the gym every mon wed and fri
  • make habitbot better every weekday
And it will just work.
